All About How To Train Your Dragon

How to Train Your Dragon is a captivating tale set in the Viking village of Berk, where dragons are considered fierce enemies. The story follows Hiccup, a clever but clumsy young Viking, who dreams of earning his place among his tribe by slaying a dragon. However, when Hiccup encounters and befriends a wounded Night Fury dragon named Toothless, he discovers that dragons are not the monsters they seem. Through their bond, Hiccup learns about trust, courage, and breaking long-standing traditions. Together, they challenge the misconceptions of their world and prove that friendship can conquer fear.
